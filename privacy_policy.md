## Introduction

Seekho Kids is an interactive educational app designed for children. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles information.

---

## Information We Do NOT Collect

Seekho Kids does **not** collect, store, or share any personal information from users, including children. Specifically, we do not collect:

- Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Photos, videos, or audio recordings
- Usage data or analytics sent to external servers
- Any data from children under 13 years of age

---

## How the App Works

Seekho Kids works **completely offline** after installation. All educational content, games, audio, and images are stored locally on the device. No data is transmitted to any server.

**Local Storage Only:** The app saves learning progress (e.g., completed levels, scores) only on the user's device using local storage. This data never leaves the device.

---

## Third-Party Services

Seekho Kids uses the following device features:

- **Text-to-Speech (TTS):** Uses the device's built-in TTS engine to speak words and sentences. No audio is recorded or transmitted.
- **Microphone (Speech Recognition):** Used optionally for voice-based activities. Audio is processed locally on the device and is never stored or transmitted.

We do **not** use any third-party advertising networks, analytics tools, or tracking services.

---

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Seekho Kids is designed for children under 13. We fully comply with the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)**:

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
- We do not show behavioral or targeted advertisements.
- Parents can delete all app data by uninstalling the app from their device.
